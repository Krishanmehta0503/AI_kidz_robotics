STEM Education For Kids

Building a Passion for Engineering, Maths and Science

Children have a natural curiosity that lends itself to science, technology, math, and engineering. At KidoBotics, we inspire kids ages 8 to 16 to build on their innate desire for answers by exploring engineering concepts in a fun, hands-on way. We closely work with schools and also provide after-school programs, evening classes, camps and special events designed to present kids with challenging yet accessible engineering activities from which they can learn and grow.
